About

About Me

I’m a software developer looking for a new role in an exciting company working with other great developers. I focus on writing accessible HTML, using modern CSS practices and writing clean JavaScript. When writing JavaScript code, I mostly use React, but I can adapt to whatever tools are required. I’m based in Akron/Cleveland, OH, but I’m happy working remotely and have experience in remote teams. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me outdoors. I love being out in nature whether that’s going for a hike, or biking the trails. I’d love you to check out my work.
